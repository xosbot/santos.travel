Our Story

Crafting Memories,
One Journey at a Time

Santos Travel is a Govt. of India Approved Inbound Tour Operator, rooted in the lush landscapes of Kerala and South India. For over two decades, we have been the trusted companion for travelers seeking authentic, immersive, and extraordinary experiences.

Our expertise spans from the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey to the misty hills of Munnar, the ancient temples of Tamil Nadu to the vibrant culture of Karnataka. Every itinerary is handcrafted with local knowledge, cultural sensitivity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
